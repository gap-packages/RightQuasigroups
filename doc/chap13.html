13.1-1 RQ_Digraph
‣ RQ_Digraph( Q )( attribute )

Returns: a directed graph on 3n+n^2 vertices, where n=|Q|. The vertices \{1,\ldots,n\}, \{n+1,\ldots,2n\}, \{2n+1,\ldots,3n\} represent the rows, columns and symbols of the multiplication table of Q. The remaining n^2 vertices stand for the triples (x,y,x*y).
